Our Facilities

Indulge in a host of exclusive benefits during your stay here at one of the top hotels in Nuwara Eliya! Enjoy a variety of facilities that will create the perfect holiday experience in Nuwara Eliya – one of the most sought after travel destinations on the island!

Luxurious Rooms

You can choose from our deluxe rooms and suites that are perfect for vacationing couples to our premium royal and presidential suites-perfect for an exclusive experience.

You can rest easy knowing that you’ve chosen from the very best abodes that Sri Lanka’s misty hill country has to offer.
