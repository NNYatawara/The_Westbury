Adventures

Nuwara Eliya offers several excursions and activities that allow you to embrace the untouched beauty of nature in the hill country of Sri Lanka.

Cook With Chef

Engage in an extraordinary culinary adventure with our hotel's bountiful organic harvest at The Westbury. Immerse yourself in a delightful recipe workshop as part of our exclusive chef program, discovering new flavors and techniques.

Royal Turf Club

The Royal Turf Club is Sri Lanka’s well-known horse racing track. Whilst visiting, you can enjoy a pony ride. In April, the prestigious Governors Cup invites spectators to cheer on for the adrenaline-filled races.

Moon Plains

Moon Plains in Nuwara Eliya, Sri Lanka, is a captivating highland plateau. Known for its moon-like terrain, it offers breathtaking panoramic views, hikes, and a unique, otherworldly landscape for nature enthusiasts.

Play Golf

The plush Nuwara Eliya Golf Course and the Victoria Golf Club in Digana provides inspiration and challenges for discerning golfers. The blanketed lawns and panoramic views from every tee position offer a content strike for golf enthusiasts.

Single Tree

Embark on a trek to the pinnacle of Single Tree Hill, which grants a breathtaking vantage point over 'The City of Lights.' The journey via Nuwara Eliya - Badulla Road encompasses lush tea plantations and captivating vistas below.

Shanthipura

Perched atop the enchanting Kikiliyamana Mountain, you'll find the island's loftiest village. Delve into the serene village life, encounter a pristine Buddhist pagoda and gain insight into fascinating cultivation practices.

The Westbury - Nuwaraeliya

Your luxury holiday in the romantically cool climes of the Hill Country is defined anew at the Palace of Sri Lanka. From the moment you depart when we would gather together to bid you adieu, your welfare and comfort are our priority
